Mental Health Tips

Practice Mindfulness

Take a few minutes each day to focus on the present moment and your breathing.

Stay Connected

Maintain regular contact with friends and family to combat feelings of isolation.

Exercise Regularly

Physical activity can boost mood and reduce stress and anxiety.
